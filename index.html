A1 is a tool to assist ‘final day’ reflections for an individual.

FAQ



How frequently should I use this tool?

Considering that this day itself could be your final day, one could reflect on a daily basis. But keeping in mind the questions for consideration, one could ideally reflect on a weekly, fortnightly, monthly, quarterly or half-yearly basis depending upon the ‘pace’ of one’s own life.

Should I share my reflections with my friends, family or community?

It is entirely upto you. Although, sharing your reflections and listening to what others have to share in a trusted space could be extremely useful.

What do you do with my written reflection?

We do not intend to and neither will we ever read and/or share it with anyone.

What is the meaning of Anant?

Anant is a word from the hindi language meaning eternal.
